Dynastes neptunus

Description

Dynastes neptunus is a beetle in the family Scarabaeidae.

Dynastes neptunus is 12 to 15.5 cm (4.7 to 6.1 inches) long with a black body. Sexual dimorphism is present in the species; for example, males having large ''horns'' on their heads and are much smoother than females.

Distribution

This beetle is usually found in the northern side of Latin America

  1. Latin America
    1. Colombia
    2. Peru
    3. Ecuador
    4. Venezuela
